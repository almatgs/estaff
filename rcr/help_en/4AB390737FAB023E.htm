Statistics

E-Staff has build-in statistical reports that allow viewing information for a single recruiter or for the entire company, for a specified period.

The reports allow users to

  • Find weaknesses and bottlenecks in the recruitment process,
  • Analyze work time for different types of vacancies,
  • Compare recruiter KPI,
  • Find the most efficient sources of candidates,
  • and much more.

You can customize existing reports and create new reports from scratch using the built-in report builder.
