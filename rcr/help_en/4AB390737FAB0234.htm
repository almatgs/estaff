Adding a vacancy manually

Vacancies are usually added manually by a recruiter.

To manually add a vacancy

  • Click New Vacancy toolbar button.

A new vacancy card opens. By default, a new vacancy has Opened state set to the current date.

  • Complete the required fields
  • Click Save and Close.

Typical mandatory fields are Name, Division (for HR departments), and Client (for recruiting agencies). After a division or client is selected, E-Staff automatically fills in the Contact persons list with the contact persons of the division or the client. You may change them later.

 When necessary, E-Staff can be integrated with a corporate Web site, so that the manager of a division is able to post a vacancy request through a Web interface.
