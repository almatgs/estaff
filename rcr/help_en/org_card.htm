Client Card

Each client card in E-Staff has the following structure:

General section:

#: Internal identification number. By default this field is filled in automatically, but you can change it. How numbers are assigned can be set up in Administration->Objects section.

Name: Name for internal use.

Full / Official Name: Name for official documents.

Alt. Name: Alternative name for search by keywords.

Address: Actual address.

Location: Region (list of values).

Phone, Fax, E-mail, Web: Common contact information, which is not connected to a specific contact person.

Contact persons: List of contact person for this company.

Industry, Size, Activity: Fields classifying a company by corresponding reference lists.

Last Contact Date: The date of last contact with this client. If the date of last contact of a contact person becomes greater than the date of last contact for the company, the latter is updated to a greater value.

Comment: Any additional information.

Events: List of events for this company.

Active Vacancies: List of active vacancies of this client.

All Vacancies: Complete list of vacancies of this client.

Candidate Events: List of events of candidates selected for one of the vacancies of this client.
