Pasting Resumes from Clipboard

Pasting resumes from  Clipboard is the most commonly used method of importing a single resume. Use Paste Resume when you have opened the resume on the screen (found on Web, opened in a text editor or in email client), and you are ready to put it into a database. E-staff automatically creates a candidate's card and parses the resume content into the appropriate fields. The source resume will be attached to the card in its original form.

To import the opened resume into the database,

  • Select and copy its content (using Copy command from the Edit menu or Ctrl+C)
  • Change to E-Staff main window
  • Click Paste Resume toolbar button.

A new candidate card opens. Appropriate fields will be extracted from the resume text, and the resume itself will be attached to the card. See Candidate Card section for more information.
